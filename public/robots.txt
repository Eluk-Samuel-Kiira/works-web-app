The most important blocks for your job board are the **filter/search URLs**. When someone visits:
```
/jobs?category=ngo&location=kampala&type=full-time
```
Google sees that as a separate page with the same job listings — duplicate content that splits your ranking power across hundreds of URL combinations instead of concentrating it on `/jobs` and individual job slugs.
